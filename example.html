Real Life xample 

ND has the best football program in the whole country, but something weird happens. There are 100 players on the team, but with only 50 helmets and jerseys available. Each player needs to put on a helmet and a jersey in order to play.

Why would it be bad:

  • There can be only 50 people at most play at the same time.
  • Each someone plays for a lot of time, then others will have no chance to play.
  • If someone enters without a helmet or jersey, he will be fined and given a penalty. :(
  • Deadlocks happen if someone has only helmet or jersey. 
  • Starvation happens if one could not get the equipments needed to play.

Soultion:

  • We can set a rule that each player plays for a certain amount of time and then gives his gears to another person, and the rotation goes on. 
  • If a player has only helmet, then he needs to pass this helmet on to someone who only has a jersey. 
  • Every ND player should play hard and respectfully.
